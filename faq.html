FAQ

Website questions UAE business owners actually ask.

Direct answers about cost, timelines, WhatsApp bookings, Arabic websites and what the monthly plan covers.

How much does a website cost for a small business in the UAE?

A small business website with AX Developments starts at AED 600 setup, then AED 399 per month for the Starter package. Growth websites start at AED 1,200 setup, then AED 549 per month, and larger booking or e-commerce systems cost more based on scope. Every package has a 3-month minimum, and the setup fee is non-refundable.

Do I need a website if I already have Instagram and WhatsApp?

Yes, a website gives your business a trusted place to send people after they find you on Instagram or WhatsApp. Instagram gets attention, but a website organizes your services, prices, reviews, location and booking action in one place. It also helps customers find you through Google instead of only social media.

How long does it take to build a website?

A Starter website can go live in about 3 days once content is ready. Growth websites usually take about 5 days, while Premium booking systems can take 3 to 4 weeks. Commerce stores usually take 6 to 8 weeks because payments, products and admin workflows need more setup.

What's included in the monthly fee?

The monthly fee includes hosting, SSL, upkeep and small content updates for the package you choose. You can send changes like text, photos, prices, opening hours, gallery updates or banners through WhatsApp. Larger new pages, redesigns or new interactive features are scoped separately.

Can I keep using WhatsApp for bookings if I get a website?

Yes, you can keep WhatsApp as the main booking or inquiry channel. Starter and Growth websites are designed to make WhatsApp easier for customers to use with clear buttons, forms and service information. If you later need online payments or a full booking dashboard, that fits the Premium package.

What's the difference between a website and a Linktree?

A website builds trust, explains your offer and gives customers a complete path to book, buy or contact you. A Linktree is mostly a list of links, so it does not sell your services, show your brand properly or help much with Google search. For a serious small business, a website should be the main destination in your bio.

Do you offer Arabic-language websites?

Yes, Arabic-language versions are available as an add-on. The current add-on is AED 400 setup plus AED 75 per month for a bilingual toggle built into your site. This is useful for UAE businesses serving both Arabic and English-speaking customers.

What happens if I want to cancel?

You can cancel after the 3-month minimum period. Your setup fee is non-refundable, and your domain stays in your name. Before cancelling, we can also discuss reducing scope or moving you to a simpler maintenance arrangement if that fits better.
