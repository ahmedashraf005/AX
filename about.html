About

AX Developments builds practical websites for UAE small businesses.

AX Developments is a UAE web design agency founded by Ahmed Abdelmonem and focused on websites, booking flows, online stores and mobile apps for businesses that need customers to take action.

Who AX Developments serves

AX Developments is led by founder Ahmed Abdelmonem and serves UAE small businesses such as cafes, salons, clinics, cake shops, florists, barbershops, restaurants, car detailing businesses and pet groomers. These businesses usually already have attention on Instagram or WhatsApp, but they need a clearer place to show services, prices, reviews, location and booking actions.

The work is built around practical conversion, not decorative pages. A useful website should make a customer trust the business faster, understand the offer clearly and contact, book or order without confusion.

How the demo-first process works

The AX Developments process starts by studying the business, its Instagram presence, its Google visibility and its competitors before the first conversation. When AX reaches out, the goal is to show a working demo link rather than a static mockup or a vague proposal.

After the business reviews the demo, AX refines the site around the real brand, photos, menu, services and customer journey. The site then goes live on the business owner's domain, with hosting, SSL, Google submission and monthly upkeep handled as part of the package.

What makes the approach different

AX Developments focuses on small UAE businesses that need a fast, low-friction way to become more credible online. The packages are designed around common business stages: a simple one-page website, a multi-page Growth site, a Premium booking system, a Commerce store or a mobile app.

The monthly model exists because UAE businesses change offers, photos, prices, hours and promotions often. Instead of leaving the owner with static files, AX keeps the site updated through WhatsApp-first communication and ongoing support.
