Monthly plans

What's included in a monthly website plan vs a one-time build?

A one-time build gives you files. A monthly website plan gives you a live site, hosting, support and a team that keeps it useful as your business changes.

A monthly website plan keeps your site live, updated and supported

A monthly website plan includes the website plus the ongoing work needed to keep it online, current and useful. With AX Developments, packages include hosting, SSL, mobile-first design and monthly upkeep based on the tier.

A one-time build can work for businesses with an internal technical person. Most small businesses in the UAE prefer a monthly plan because they can send updates on WhatsApp instead of managing hosting, domains, files and fixes.

What the monthly plan covers

The monthly plan covers the practical work that happens after launch. That includes keeping the site hosted, secure and updated with current business information.

  • Hosting and SSL
  • Small text, photo, price and opening-hours updates
  • Gallery, form or banner updates depending on the package
  • WhatsApp-first support
  • Search Console and analytics setup on Growth and above
  • Ongoing upkeep so the site does not become outdated

What is not included by default?

New pages, full redesigns, major layout changes and new interactive features are not included by default. Those changes affect the structure of the site, so they need a separate scope.

For example, changing opening hours is upkeep. Adding a full online payment booking flow is a new feature and belongs in a Premium scope.

Why not just do a one-time build?

A one-time build is fine if the site will rarely change and someone on your side can handle technical work. But most small businesses change offers, photos, menus, prices and hours often.

The monthly model keeps the website alive as the business changes. It also keeps responsibility clear: you focus on running the business, and AX Developments handles the website.

What are the minimum terms?

AX Developments packages have a 3-month minimum. The setup fee is non-refundable, and the domain stays in your name.

If you want Arabic as part of the site, the bilingual add-on is AED 400 setup plus AED 75 per month.
