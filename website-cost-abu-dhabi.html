Website cost

How much does a website cost for a small business in Abu Dhabi?

A small business website in Abu Dhabi should be priced around what it actually needs to do: build trust, explain the offer and turn visitors into WhatsApp inquiries, bookings or orders.

Website pricing in Abu Dhabi starts at AED 600 setup

With AX Developments, a small business website starts at AED 600 setup, then AED 399 per month. That Starter package is built for businesses that need a clean one-page site with services, photos, location, opening hours and WhatsApp contact.

If the business needs more pages, forms, reviews, directory setup and basic SEO, the Growth package starts at AED 1,200 setup, then AED 549 per month. More advanced systems, like online booking with payments or e-commerce, start from AED 4,000+ or AED 8,000+ setup depending on the scope.

What changes the price?

The price changes when the website needs more than a simple marketing page. A salon with a few services and WhatsApp booking is very different from a clinic with treatment pages, doctor profiles, forms and paid bookings.

  • Number of pages and sections
  • Booking forms, quote forms or online payments
  • Product catalog, cart and checkout requirements
  • Arabic-language version or bilingual toggle
  • Admin dashboard, database or customer accounts
  • How much content needs to be written, organized or cleaned up

What should Abu Dhabi businesses avoid?

Avoid paying for a website that looks good but gives customers no clear next step. The site should make it obvious how to book, call, message, visit or order.

Also avoid building everything as a one-time file with no plan for updates. UAE small businesses change prices, offers, photos and opening hours often, so the monthly upkeep matters if you do not want stale information online.

Which AX package fits which business?

Premium fits businesses that need real booking, payments, a database or a dashboard. Commerce fits restaurants and retailers that want their own store instead of relying only on delivery apps or marketplaces.
